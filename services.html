Fees / Pricing

Direct Taxes:

Preparing and E-filing periodic withholding tax statements under section 165 of Income Tax Ordinance, 2001, (ITO’2001) upon management’s review, consideration and approval of such tax statements.
Preparing and E-filing of annual withholding tax statements, upon management’s review, consideration and approval of such tax statements.
Preparing and E-filing of Annual Income Tax Return, upon management’s review, consideration and approval of such Tax Return.
Preparation and E-filling of quarterly advance tax return with tax authorities under section 147 ITO’2001.
Advising management on the queries raised with regard to deduction of taxes under the different provision of the ITO’2001.
Advising management in their efforts for compliance with notices issued by the tax authorities from time to time on different matters.

Indirect Taxes

Preparing and E-filing of monthly sales tax return, upon management’s review, consideration and approval of such tax return under section 26 of Sales Tax Act’ 1990 (STA’1990)
Advising management on the queries raised with regard to deduction of taxes under the withholding provision of the STA’1990.
Advising management in their efforts for compliance with notices issued by the tax authorities from time to time on different matters.

Annual Income Tax Return:

Preparing and E-filing of Annual Income Tax Return of the Company, upon management’s review, consideration and approval of such Tax Return.
Preparing and E-filing of annual individual return of the directors of the company
Preparing and E-filing of annual individual return of the employees of the company

Corporate Compliance

Filing of Form-A and 29
Issuance of Certified True Copies (CTC) when needed
